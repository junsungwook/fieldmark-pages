Fieldmark Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Fieldmark is an offline-first inspection workflow app for solo home inspectors. This policy explains what information the app stores and how it is used.

Information You Enter

Fieldmark stores inspection information that you choose to enter or attach in the app, including:

  • Inspection address and inspection date
  • Section notes and review status
  • Defect titles, severity, notes, and reusable comments
  • Inspection photos, captions, and photo links to defects
  • Local backup and report export files that you create

Camera and Photo Library Access

Fieldmark requests camera and photo library access so you can attach inspection photos to report sections and defects. Photos are used for inspection documentation and local report output.

Local Storage

Fieldmark is local-first. Inspection data is stored on your device by default. PDF, HTML, text, JSON backups, and photo-inclusive ZIP backups are created only when you choose to export, save, or share them. JSON backups contain photo metadata, while ZIP backups may include inspection photo files that exist on the device.

Accounts and Server Transfer

The current version of Fieldmark does not require account creation or login. It does not automatically send inspection data to a Fieldmark server. If optional cloud backup or sync is added in the future, this policy will be updated to explain how that feature works.

Subscriptions and Purchase Processing

Builds that enable Fieldmark Pro subscriptions use the App Store and RevenueCat to process subscription purchases, restores, and entitlement status. Purchase status, receipt validation information, app instance identifiers, and store or device technical information may be used for subscription processing. Inspection addresses, defect notes, inspection photos, and report contents are not sent to RevenueCat for subscription processing.

Tracking and Advertising

Fieldmark does not use third-party advertising networks, advertising tracking, or data collection for AI defect diagnosis.

Data Sharing

Fieldmark does not sell your inspection data. If you share a PDF, HTML, text, JSON, or ZIP backup file, that sharing is controlled by you. Review the file before sharing because it may contain addresses, notes, and inspection photos.

Data Deletion

You can delete local inspection data in the app. If you delete the app, locally stored Fieldmark data is removed according to the operating system's app deletion behavior.

Contact

For privacy questions, email Fieldmark support.

Fieldmark is an offline-first inspection workflow app built for solo home inspectors. Capture defects, attach photo evidence, reuse trusted comments, and check report readiness while you are still on-site.

Fast field capture

Add defects with snippets, severity, notes, and photo evidence without turning the field workflow into office work.

Offline-first

Inspection data stays local on the device until you export or back it up.

Report readiness

Review missing photos, notes, and unfinished sections before leaving the property.
